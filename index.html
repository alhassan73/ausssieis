Our Services

Oceanfreight

Ocean transport is a vital part of the world economy. This is why the seafreight department of Aussie integrated Solutions provides FCL, LCL as well as break-bulk service from any port in the world to Egyptian ports and vice versa. Our service is by no means concluded once the shipment reaches its port of destination. Aussie I.L.S. provides highly capable and adaptable transport management from door-to-door. And it goes without saying - we provide all necessary associated services, from standard documentation to complex banking arrangements and marine insurance coverage .

Airfreight

Aussie I.L.S. will be your strong partner in transporting your goods even to the remotest corners of the earth. The extensive transport links we offer based on our cooperation with first-class carriers means you enjoy maximum global presence. And we can save you time and money. For each shipment and each delivery we prepare the air transport systems that meet our customers' specific needs. Negotiating our air freight globally through associated agencies assures us permanent goodwill and best contacts.

Inland Haulage

We ensure that your products are moved at the right time to the right place. Whatever your need for inland transportation, you can count on us to deliver an effective and cost efficient inland haulage service in the most timely and secure manner possible. We coordinate FCL trucking activities for our customers. We use high-quality LTL trucking partners that provide the fastest transit at the most affordable price.

Consolidation

When you don't have enough cargo for a full container, rely on us to provide you with LCL departures across the world. Aussie I.L.S. has a convincing gateway concept ready for you through weekly consolidation less than-container-load (LCL) services, ranging from the hardly accessible sub ports to the major hub ports. Linder the full control of Aussie I.L.S., we offer the best connectivity and price, while increasing cargo safety and reducing transit times by eliminating conventional transshipment points. Development in Aussie I.L.S. combines volume that has been progressing well and we're further expanding our network by adding additional destinations.

Warehousing and Distribution

Thanks to our experienced specialists and advanced IT systems, Aussie I.L.S. has opportunities to offer a wide range of warehousing and distribution services. Aussie’s logistics solutions are tailor-made which means that to each customer we prepare individual logistic offer. We offer warehousing facilities that comfortably accommodate multi- and single users. Irrespective of the size of their companies, the scope of services we provide to them, the storage space they require or the length of their contract with us, our customers enjoy full access to years of international and local supply chain management at all levels.

Project Logistics

Aussie I.L.S. provides professional and comprehensive services to meet all requirements for a successful project completion. Every Industrial project is unique, as is every logistics solutions connected with it, that’s why Aussie I.L.S. has introduced transportation, handling and positioning of heavy lifts Understanding the growing needs of medium phased industrial expansion in Egypt, in terms of power projects, oil resources, petrochemical, desalination and other industrial sectors, Aussie I.L.S. will define the best combination of air, ocean and overland transport to suit your project needs.

Customs Brokerage

With offices directly linked up to the customs authorities via a data network, Aussie I.L.S. is able to offer comprehensive customs-and-quarantine clearance for your import and export cargo. As a licensed customs agent, Aussie I.L.S. provides all types of customs clearance operations and offers a wide range of services and assistance to simplify customs procedures: Implementation of simplified procedures, ability to obtain all requested documents (licenses, authorizations,..), relations with various administrations (veterinary, phytosanitary,..), customs clearance of vehicles and works of art.

Cross-trade

Aussie I.L.S. offers a geographically elaborate cross-trade. We provide cross-trading at competitive rates, flawless documentation, infallible transportation and spot-on delivery for shipments. At all times, we do our best to work in the best interest of our customers. Our Cross Trading offerings cover a wide geographical region. We offer very innovative cross trading services to our customers, backed up by complete coordination between the origin, destination and cross trading point.

Courier

Parcels, packages and courier packs are our business. We move them all nationwide through Aussie I.L.S.’s extensive network. Delivering in full, on time, and undamaged is our main goal. We also pride ourselves in our ability to save time, with a mission to provide cost effective services for our clients and keep them fully informed at all times. Aussie I.L.S. can offer an alternative to the high cost of shipping courier packages based on our volume discounts, with the world's leading courier companies .

E-commerce

Information technology is one of the most powerful enablers that Aussie I.L.S. has at his disposal, and it is rapidly changing the way he operates. Armed with real-time communication and information sharing capabilities, inventory status, goods efficiency reports, and track-and-trace capabilities, the e-competent Aussie I.L.S. can approach his customer with a reservoir of services and options that let him blend the virtual world with the physical one .

Network

Aussie I.L.S. relies on an established global network of national and international partners and agents that create a physical presence at every major port and airport in the world to provide fully automated processing of air and ocean freight movements .
