About Us

Who we are

Aussie's superior service and integrated end-to-end supply chain management solutions help our customers turn their logistics challenges into a real competitive advantage. Aussie is an international freight forwarder specialized in providing integrated supply chain solutions for a comprehensive range of industries. With its global network in more than 100 countries, Aussie provides: · Seafreight: leader in seafreight, Aussie offers comprehensive seafreight services via a worldwide and independent network focused on FCL and LCL shipments, break bulk, project logistics and oversized transport. · Airfreight: becoming one of the largest airfreight forwarding specialists is our top priority by offering a comprehensive range of international air cargo solutions and unbeatable levels of customer service. · Road and Rail Logistics: catering integrated road and rail offerings; extensive and growing global network, and relationships with best-in-class carriers. · Contract Logistics: dedicated teams design solutions for all aspects of transportation management, contract logistics, or an integration of both teams to deliver quantifiable efficiencies to your supply chain. We deliver these services leveraging internationally standardized, leading-edge information technology, ensuring synchronized display and control of each transport movement across the supply chain. We work to turn our customers' logistics into a competitive advantage.

Why Us

The reason behind Aussie I.L.S.'s success in becoming a leader within the industry: That our service is by no means finished once the shipment reaches its port of destination. Aussie I.L.S. has first class connections everywhere, providing high capable and flexible multi-modal transport management from door to door. It goes without saying- all the necessary associated services, from packing via documentation up to insurance are all included .

Our Vision

Customers trust Aussie I.L.S. as the preferred logistics partner, leading the industry in terms of quality, profitability and market share. Philosophy of our company is to aim fully at meeting the present and future needs of our customers, to improve quality of work and to enlarge service portfolio. Definite emphasis is placed on long-term cooperation with customers and solution of their requirements by individual approach to achieve loyalty of such customers.

Our Mission

Our aim is to offer a one-stop-shop for all the trading public. Whether it is imports or exports, locally or worldwide we will offer an unsurpassed level of service at the most competitive rates, fit to meet the expectations and requirements of our clients.
